About

Hi!

This project is to show you photos from far and not so far countries. In any case, pictures on this site do not pretend to be professional photos or photos for exhibition. This is my hobby, and this site is only one of ways to help me to transform photos from film into images. If you casually come across to my site, please have a look at it. It's pleasant to me, if someone except me like these pictures.
